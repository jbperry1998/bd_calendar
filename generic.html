Why did we start HooCooks?

We saw the need to teach college kids not just how to cook, but how to budget, groceryshop, and create a balanced diet all on their own.

James Perry (co-founder)

"We just wanted to show our peers that, like they say in Ratatouille, anyone can cook. Not only can anyone cook, but cookig for yourself can actually be a cheaper and healthier option than most people realize"
